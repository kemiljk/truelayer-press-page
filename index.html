Instant

Payments go directly in your bank account, so you can access funds immediately.

Secure

Authentication is handled by your user’s bank, reducing fraud risk and increasing success rates.

Cost-effective

Eliminate expensive Card and Direct Debit fees and pay a low fixed-cost per transaction.

Flexible
